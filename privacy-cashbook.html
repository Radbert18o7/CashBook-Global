Privacy Policy

CashBook Global — Free Cashbook App for Small Businesses

Last updated: March 31, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to CashBook Global, a free financial cashbook application developed and operated by Rahul Jogi ("we", "our", or "us"), based in Canada. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application and web service (collectively, the "Service").

By using CashBook Global, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Service.

Our commitment: We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our Service. We never sell your personal data to third parties. Your financial data belongs to you.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: Name, email address, and profile photo when you register via Google Sign-In or email/password
  • Business information: Business name, address, currency preference, and timezone
  • Financial records: Cash in/out entries, amounts, dates, categories, payment modes, remarks, and contact information you enter into the app
  • Team information: Email addresses of team members you invite to share books
  • Voice data: When you use the voice-to-text feature, audio is processed locally on your device using your device's built-in speech recognition. We do not store or transmit your audio recordings to our servers.
  • Images: Receipt photos and business logos you upload are stored securely via Cloudinary

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: App features used, screens visited, buttons tapped (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, app version, language settings
  • Crash reports: Error logs and crash data to help us fix bugs (via Firebase Crashlytics) — these never include your financial data
  • Performance data: App load times and feature performance metrics (via Firebase Performance Monitoring)
